Welcome to PragAgro

At PragAgro, we are dedicated to making agriculture in Tanzania simpler, more practical and sustainable. Our mission is to empower farmers with simple, innovative solutions that increase productivity and sustainability.

About Us

PragAgro is a Tanzania-based company founded by a team of passionate individuals who met during their studies in the Netherlands. Driven by a shared vision, we are dedicated to transforming agriculture in Tanzania through practical and sustainable solutions.

Our Story

PragAgro was founded with a simple mission: to make agriculture more efficient, sustainable, and profitable. Born out of a passion for farming and a drive to solve real-world challenges, we’ve grown into a trusted partner for farmers and agribusinesses. Our journey began with a single question: How can we make farming simpler and more practical? Today, we’re proud to offer cutting-edge tools, resources, and expertise that help farmers thrive in an ever-changing world.

Our Mission

At PragAgro, our mission is to provide simply practical solutions that transform the way agriculture works. We believe in empowering farmers with tools and knowledge that are easy to use, effective, and sustainable. By bridging the gap between tradition and innovation, we’re helping to build a brighter future for agriculture.

Our Values

  • Practicality: We focus on solutions that are easy to implement and deliver real results.
  • Sustainability: We’re committed to practices that protect the environment and ensure long-term success.
  • Innovation: We embrace technology and creativity to solve the toughest challenges in agriculture.
